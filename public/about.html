About Me

back to homepage

Front-end developer from Montreal, Canada, based in Europe

    My introduction to coding took place thanks to my daughter. Knowing that I was looking for a new challenge, she suggested I took a look at SheCodes coding courses. I had always been very curious about coding and although I found it incredible challenging, or perhaps because I did, I inscribed, celebrating the feeling of now having a clue about what it'd be about.
    Because of the vast experience I have in different working fields, I usually know something of any topic I come across. But things were different this time, and a subtle memory of a excursion I once enrolled in to the Pyramids, and ended up with a bus full of Swedish tourists, came to mind. It is very rare that I don't understand a word foreigners are saying, but this time, I not only didn't understand sounds either. Only one sound stuck, as I heard it many times, and apparently it meant "but". Does it say something about the Swedish culture? We'll discuss that another time. For now, SheCodes, and the free introduction course from the hand of Matt Delac are inspring me a lot...
